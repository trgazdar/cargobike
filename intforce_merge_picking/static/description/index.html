Merge Multiple Delivery Orders / Incoming Shipments

In case of lots of picking delivery / incoming shipment /receipts, It's very hard to manage them as it is very difficult for warehouse person to open each delivery/shipment/receipts and process each one of them one by one.
Here intforce is presenting an app which can reduce the time and work of warehouse user who can merge the delivery orders and incoming shipment/receipts.
