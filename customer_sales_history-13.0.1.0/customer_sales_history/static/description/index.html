Sales History in order and customer

This module help to see sales history in sale order and customer.

Features

  • Sales history from sale order view.
  • Sales history from customer view.
  • Community & Enterprise

View Sales History From Sale Order

  • Sales history Smart button in Sale order detailed view.


    • when user click history smart button, then below view of History(old) Sales Order of related customer will open.


    View Sales History From Customer

  • Sales history Smart button in customer(partner) detailed view.


    • when user click history smart button, then below view of History(old) Sales Order of related customer will open.
